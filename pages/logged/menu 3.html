Buy, Sell & Rent

Buy-Sell-Rent constitutes a major portion of our Portfolio Management Services. Under the Buy-Sell-Rent, we at Tejasco provide assistance across a wide array of services, entailed across the transactions pertaining to Buying, Selling, and Renting of a Real Estate Asset/Property.

Buy

Our repertoire spans from identification of a potential property to vetting it for compliance, seeking legal clearances, exploring financing options etc… all aligned to the specific needs of our customer. We strive to simplify the process for our customers; there by facilitating their decision making process and helping them realize the dream of owning a home.

Sell

Our access to information at various levels and market intelligence inputs from reliable sources acts as a catalyst to our advisory on Selling decisions. A good sale is not just about getting a potential customer but knowing the right time to sell thus, making Real Estate Wealth grow. Apart from advising the customers on the right time to sell their asset, we also help identify potential buyers, run a background check on the authenticity of the buyer, negotiate on your behalf and front end all the necessary paper work and legal formalities as also assist the potential buyer with financing options for timely closures.

Rent

We at Tejasco understand how important it is to have your asset generate revenue thus, our service to assist with ‘Renting’ of your property. Unlike the local brokers who focus only on identifying a tenant, we at Tejasco, handle the process of property renting, end-to-end. From identifying a potential tenant, running background checks for their reliability, negotiating on your behalf to create a win-win, formulating a legally viable rental agreement clearly stating provisions protecting your property, to taking charge of all the necessary Maintenance works for the property, ensuring continuity of occupancy, we have it all covered.
